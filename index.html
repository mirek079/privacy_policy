What User Data We Collect

When you use our App, we do not collect or store any types personal of data. When using multiplayer mode the gamer is being logged to cloud server using one of four predefined accounts. The account is assigned based sequentially. Server doesn’t collect any data except for the one that are necessary to play the game, those are Your and your opponents movements. None of your personal information is being send to the server.

Permissions and Device Access

Our App may request access to certain features of your device, including:

  • Camera & Photos: For downloading photo to use as an avatar in game.
  • Internet connection: For multiplayer mode.
You can enable or disable these permissions through your device settings.
